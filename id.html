+

Berapa biayanya?

+
Card creation is $0 (normally $9.99). First month maintenance is free. Top-ups are free (minus gas fees). FX conversion fee is 1%, and top-up conversion is 0.5%. See our website for full fee details.

Di mana saya bisa menggunakan Fizen Card?

+
You can use Fizen Card anywhere Visa is accepted, including 150M+ merchants across 200+ countries. You can also add it to Apple Pay and Google Pay for contactless payments.

Apakah Fizen Card aman?

+
Yes. Fizen Card uses industry-standard security with SSL encryption, 3D Secure verification, and is backed by Tether. Your card is virtual and can be frozen instantly if needed. Fizen never stores sensitive data on your device.
